SAVOR JAPAN!

KareRa(カス)

Japanese curry is a thick, mildly spiced dish made with a roux-based sauce, simmered with potatoes, carrots, onions, and meat, then served over steamed rice or with crispy katsu.

Tempra(天ら)

Tempura is a light, crispy Japanese dish of seafood or vegetables dipped in a delicate batter and deep-fried, often served with tentsuyu dipping sauce, grated daikon, or sprinkled with salt.

Unagi (うなぎ)

Unagi(うなぎ) is freshwater eel and a beloved delicacy in Japanese cuisine. It is typically grilled over charcoal and glazed with a sweet-savory tare sauce made of soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and sake, giving it a rich, caramelized flavor.

Kakiōi(き氷)

Kakigōri is a fluffy, snow-like Japanese shaved ice dessert topped with colorful syrups such as matcha, strawberry, or mango, often enhanced with condensed milk, mochi, or red bean paste.

Takyki(たこ)

Takoyaki are crispy, golden-brown octopus-filled balls made from wheat flour batter, cooked in special molds, and topped with savory sauce, mayonnaise, bonito flakes, and seaweed powder

Sushi (寿司)

Sushi is an iconic Japanese dish of vinegared rice paired with fresh seafood, vegetables, or egg, served in various styles like nigiri, maki, and chirashi, often with soy sauce and wasabi.
